# Sales Performance Analytics for Retail & E-Commerce

## 📌 Executive Summary
Using SQL and Power BI, this project analyzes internet sales data to build an interactive dashboard that tracks sales performance across customers, products, and time. The analysis highlights key insights such as top-performing products, high-value customers, and monthly sales trends, along with a comparison of actual sales against budget targets. This enables stakeholders to quickly evaluate performance, identify growth opportunities, and make data-driven business decisions through dynamic filtering and visual insights.

## 📌 Business Problem
Sales performance is a key revenue driver, yet the organization relies on static, Excel-based reporting that lacks scalability and analytical depth. This limits visibility into critical dimensions such as customer behavior, product performance, and time-based trends, restricting holistic business insights. The absence of an integrated reporting framework makes it difficult to track KPIs and identify revenue-driving factors. Additionally, the lack of budget vs actual comparison prevents effective performance benchmarking and variance analysis. As a result, stakeholders face challenges in deriving actionable insights, leading to delayed decision-making and missed optimization opportunities.

**How can the business leverage data to gain real-time visibility, track performance against targets, and enable faster, data-driven decision-making?**

---

## 📌 Methodology
- Extracted, cleaned, and transformed sales data from the SQL database using T-SQL to prepare structured, analysis-ready datasets.  
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze sales performance across customer, product, time, and geographic dimensions.  
- Developed KPIs and measures to track sales trends and perform budget vs actual comparison, enabling data-driven insights.

## 📌 Results & Business Recommendations

The developed dashboard provides a centralized and interactive view of sales performance, enabling stakeholders to monitor key metrics across customers, products, time, and geography. By consolidating data into a single reporting layer, it improves visibility into revenue drivers, highlights top-performing segments, and allows comparison of actual sales against budget. This reduces dependency on static reports and enables faster, data-driven decision-making through real-time insights and dynamic filtering.

### 📊 Key Insights
- A small set of products contributes significantly to overall revenue (Top 10 dominance)  
- Certain customers drive a major portion of sales, indicating high-value segments  
- Sales trends show fluctuations across months, highlighting seasonality patterns  
- Variance between actual sales and budget indicates areas of over/under-performance  

### 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing products and optimize inventory and marketing strategies to maximize revenue contribution.  
- Strengthen engagement with high-value customers through targeted campaigns and personalized offers.  
- Investigate underperforming products and regions to identify potential gaps and improvement opportunities.  
- Continuously monitor sales vs budget performance to improve forecasting accuracy and strategic planning.  
- Leverage dashboard insights for proactive decision-making rather than reactive reporting.
